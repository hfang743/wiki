    Motor type Brushless servo motor
    Voltage range 24~45 VDC
    Gear ratio 6:1

    Interface Isolated CAN

    Ambient temperature range -20~+80 °C
    Noise level <= 70 dB(A)

    Length 37mm
    Weight 453.1 g
    Backlash 9 Arc min
    Static load capacity 1270N
